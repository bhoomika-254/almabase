# EduPlatform - Company Overview

## About EduPlatform

EduPlatform is a cloud-based Learning Management System (LMS) designed to serve higher education institutions worldwide. Founded in 2015 and headquartered in Austin, Texas, EduPlatform partners with over 50 universities across 15 countries, supporting more than 500,000 active students and 35,000 faculty members.

## Mission

Our mission is to make quality education accessible and manageable through technology — empowering institutions to deliver engaging, measurable, and compliant learning experiences at scale.

## Core Products

### EduPlatform LMS Core
The primary platform offering course creation, content delivery, assignment management, grading, and learner progress tracking. Available as a SaaS solution with 99.9% uptime SLA.

### EduPlatform Analytics
A real-time analytics engine providing institutional dashboards, student performance tracking, early warning systems for at-risk students, and accreditation reporting tools.

### EduPlatform Virtual Classroom
Integrated live video lecture capabilities supporting up to 500 concurrent students per session, with recording, transcription, and accessibility features.

## Clients & Scale

EduPlatform serves institutions ranging from community colleges to R1 research universities. Notable client categories include:

- 22 state universities in the United States

- 8 community college systems
- 8 international universities (UK, Canada, Australia, India)

Annual transaction volumes: over 12 million course enrollments, 85 million assignment submissions, and 2.4 billion page views processed per year.

## Technology Infrastructure

EduPlatform runs entirely on Amazon Web Services (AWS), leveraging multi-region deployment for high availability and disaster recovery. Our primary data centers are located in US-East (Virginia) and US-West (Oregon), with a failover region in EU-West (Ireland) for international clients.
