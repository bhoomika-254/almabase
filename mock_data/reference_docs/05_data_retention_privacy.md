# EduPlatform - Data Retention & Privacy Policy

## Introduction

This policy governs how EduPlatform collects, processes, retains, and deletes data on behalf of educational institutions. EduPlatform acts as a **data processor** under GDPR and a **service provider** under applicable U.S. state privacy laws.

## Types of Data Collected

### Institutional Data
- Institution name, address, billing information, and administrator contacts.
- Custom configuration settings and branding.
- Course catalog and curriculum information.

### User Data
- Student: name, email, institutional ID, enrollment records, grades, submissions, activity logs.
- Faculty: name, email, institutional ID, course assignments, grading activity.
- Administrators: name, email, system access logs.

### Technical & Usage Data
- IP addresses, browser type, operating system.
- Login timestamps, session duration, pages visited.
- Feature usage telemetry (aggregated and anonymized for product improvement).

## Data Retention Schedule

| Data Category | Retention Period | Deletion Method |
|---------------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Active student records | Duration of enrollment + 7 years post-graduation | Secure deletion (NIST 800-88) |
| Withdrawn student records | 5 years from withdrawal date | Secure deletion |
| Faculty records | Duration of employment + 3 years | Secure deletion |
| Audit/security logs | 7 years | Secure deletion |
| System activity logs | 3 years | Automatic purge |
| Database backups | 30 days (rolling) | Automatic purge |
| Payment/billing records | 7 years (financial compliance) | Secure deletion |
| Anonymized analytics | Indefinite (non-PII) | N/A |

## GDPR Compliance

EduPlatform fully supports institutions' GDPR obligations for EU-based students:

- **Lawful Basis:** EduPlatform processes data under "legitimate interests" and "contract performance" as processor.
- **Data Subject Rights:** EduPlatform supports institution workflows for data access, rectification, erasure, and portability requests.
- **Right to Erasure:** Upon verified student request through the institution, PII is deleted within 30 days.
- **Data Portability:** Student data exports (CSV, JSON) are available through the administrator portal.
- **Data Processing Agreement (DPA):** Available to all EU institutions. Includes Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for cross-border transfers.
- **Data Protection Officer (DPO):** EduPlatform's DPO can be reached at dpo@eduplatform.com.

## COPPA Compliance

For institutions serving users under 13 years of age (K-12 partnerships and dual enrollment programs):

- EduPlatform collects only data necessary for the educational service.
- Verifiable parental consent mechanisms are provided when required.
- Children's data is never used for advertising or behavioral profiling.
- Data collected from users under 13 is deleted within 30 days of account closure.

## Cross-Border Data Transfers

- US institutions' data is stored and processed exclusively in US-based AWS regions (US-East-1, US-West-2).
- EU institutions' data is stored and processed in the EU-West-1 (Ireland) AWS region.
- Cross-border data transfers to EduPlatform's US headquarters for support purposes are covered by Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs).

## Data Minimization

EduPlatform adheres to the principle of data minimization:
- We collect only the data necessary to provide contracted services.
- We do not sell or monetize student data.
- We do not use student data for targeted advertising.
- Third-party integrations are vetted and are only permitted access to the data they need.

## Data Deletion Process

Upon contract termination:
1. Institution submits a written deletion request.
2. EduPlatform begins deletion within 30 days.
3. All backups containing institutional data are purged within 90 days.
4. A Certificate of Data Deletion is provided to the institution.
